Arambh Demo Video Script (90 seconds)

---

SCENE 1 — Logo & Tagline (0–10s)

Visual:
- Soft gradient background in warm orange tones.
- Arambh logo animates in the center with a subtle glow.

Voiceover:
- "Meet Arambh — the modern training platform for Indian businesses."

On-screen text:
- "Arambh"
- Subheading: "The modern training platform for Indian businesses"

---

SCENE 2 — Admin Uploads Training Video (10–25s)

Visual:
- Cut to the Arambh admin dashboard.
- Cursor drags and drops a training video file into a large upload card.
- Progress bar smoothly animates from 0% to 100%.
- The file appears instantly in a list of training videos.

Voiceover:
- "Admins can upload SOPs and training videos in seconds. Just drag, drop, and let Arambh handle the rest."

On-screen text callout:
- "Drag & Drop Uploader"
- "Instant Processing"

---

SCENE 3 — Employee Sees & Watches Training (25–40s)

Visual:
- Phone mockup slides in from the side, showing the `/learn` page.
- Employee receives a notification banner: "New training assigned: Customer Success 101".
- They tap into the course, see a clean course card, and hit play.
- A modern video player fills the screen with playback controls.

Voiceover:
- "Employees instantly see new trainings on their /learn page, and watch them in a beautiful, distraction-free player."

On-screen text callout:
- "Mobile-first learning"
- "Clean video experience"

---

SCENE 4 — XP, Badges & Leaderboard (40–55s)

Visual:
- As the video reaches the end, a bright XP burst animation explodes around the completion button.
- A badge popup appears: "Completed: Customer Success 101".
- Cut to a leaderboard screen where the employee’s avatar moves up a few positions.

Voiceover:
- "Completion triggers XP, badges, and live leaderboards — turning everyday training into a game your team actually wants to win."

On-screen text callout:
- "XP & Badges"
- "Live Leaderboard"

---

SCENE 5 — Ask AI About Training (55–70s)

Visual:
- Split-screen: on the left, the training video thumbnail; on the right, an AI chat panel.
- Employee types: "How should I respond if a customer wants a discount?"
- AI response animates line by line, highlighting key phrases.
- Small tooltip shows: "Source: Customer Success 101 · Chapter 3".

Voiceover:
- "When someone has a question, Arambh AI answers using your actual training content — so every reply matches your company’s playbook."

On-screen text callout:
- "AI trained on YOUR content"
- "Instant, company-specific answers"

---

SCENE 6 — Admin Sees Real-time Reports (70–90s)

Visual:
- Back to the admin dashboard, now on the Reports page.
- Graphs and completion percentages animate into view.
- A table shows employees with completion status and XP.
- Key metric cards highlight: "92% Completed", "Avg Time to Complete: 18 mins".

Voiceover:
- "Admins get real-time visibility into who&apos;s completed what, where people are stuck, and which trainings drive real performance."

On-screen text callout:
- "Track every employee's progress, live"

Final frame:
- The screen fades to the Arambh brand lockup.

Voiceover:
- "Arambh — give your team the brain of your entire company."

On-screen end card:
- "arambh.com"
- Button-style text: "Start Free"
